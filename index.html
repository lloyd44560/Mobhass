DOOR (RFID TECHNOLOGY)

We are removing the traditional way of accessing your door using an RFID (Radio-frequency identification) door locking system that offers more security and convenience to your home. MOBHASS featured a fixed RFID reader, which is commonly mounted on walls and is attached to a given location. To unlock and open the door, the household member simply taps his or her registered key card into the RFID reader. Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) is the non-contact wireless transmission of data using radiofrequency waves. In an RFID door lock access control system, each household member's credential (typically a keycard or fob with an RFID chip) contains unique identifying information called a tag.

About us

MOBHASS is an IoT Based Home Security System where users may remotely monitor and control virtually the IoT devices in their houses, which can help them avoid home-related incidents. Smart houses technologies significantly collect and analyze real-time data to improve home energy efficiency and sustainability as well as residents' security, comfort, and safety.


MOBHASS is a group of students founded concurrently in 2021 for their Capstones 1 and 2. The team considers that we are in an era where almost everything can be controlled with a single click or touch. The group aims to design an Internet of Things-based home security system that can be managed and monitored using our smartphones.
